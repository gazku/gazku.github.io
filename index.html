About the Book

The book Steve Jobs by Jessie Hartland explores the life of Steve Jobs, the co-founder of Apple, the famous computer company. He was also a popular figure in technology, having inventing a Apple-branded smart-phone in 2007 (called the iPhone {The "i" standing for innovative}).

Fun Facts

  • Steve Jobs was born on February 24, 1955, was put up for adoption, and then adopted by Paul and Clara Jobs shortly afterwards.
  • He co-founded Apple in 1976 with Steve Wozniak in his parents' garage. He had also invented the Apple I, and demonstrated it at a local tech club.
  • Jobs was known for his very strict attention to detail and was a perfectionist in his tech design.
