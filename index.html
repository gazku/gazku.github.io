About the Book

The book *Steve Jobs* by Jessie Hartland explores the incredible life of Steve Jobs, the co-founder of Apple Inc., and an influential figure in technology and innovation. This graphic biography provides insights into his journey from a college dropout to a visionary leader in the tech world.

Fun Facts

  • Steve Jobs was born on February 24, 1955, and adopted by Paul and Clara Jobs shortly afterward.
  • He co-founded Apple in 1976 with Steve Wozniak in his parents' garage.
  • Jobs was known for his attention to detail and was a perfectionist in his product designs.
